Sweet Potato Boats

  • 2 sweet potato
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup blanched broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • salt as required
  • black pepper as required
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 handful coriander leaves

How to make Sweet Potato Boats

Step 1: Soak sweet potatoes

To make this easy sweet potato boat, wash and soak the sweet potatoes in lukewarm water, then rinse it off through cold water.

Step 2: Make cavity

Next, cut the sweet potatoes and using a spoon scoop out sweet potatoes and create a cavity. Take the sweet potato extracts in a bowl, add in crumbled cottage cheese, blanched broccoli, and chickpeas mix them well.

Step 3: Bake and enjoy

Then add in chopped coriander leaves, pepper, red chilies and 1 tbsp olive oil. Toss it up well. Place the mixture in the cavity and air fry/bake for 20-25 minutes at 180 degree celsius. Enjoy.
