Creamy Mango Chia Pudding

Indegredients of Creamy Mango Chia Pudding

  • 2 medium ripe mangoes
  • 4 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 11/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoon flaked almonds

How to make Creamy Mango Chia Pudding

Step 1

To begin with this easy delicacy, wash and peel the mangoes. Then cut them into small chunks and make a smooth pure.

Step 2

Take a bowl and mix chia seeds with warm coconut milk, honey and a dash of cinnamon. Toss it well and soak overnight.

Step 3

Take it out in the morning and assemble the dish by pouring the fresh mango puree and topping it up with chilled coconut chia pudding and fresh mango chunks followed by almonds, repeat the layers and refrigerate for an hour to enjoy.
