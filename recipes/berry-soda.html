Berry Orange Soda

Ingredients of Berry Orange Soda

  • 2 cup orange juice
  • 500 ml sprite
  • lemon slices as required
  • mint leaves as required
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup strawberry

How to make Berry Orange Soda

Step 1

To make this easy drink, wash and cut the strawberries. Meanwhile, slice lemons and keep aside.

Step 2

Next, take serving glasses, add in the fresh strawberries, lemon slices, mint leaves and 1 tsp sugar, muddle it well.

Step 3

To the glass add some crushed ice and pour ½ cup orange juice followed by ¼ cup soda. Stir, adjust the sweetness as per taste and enjoy!
