Creamy Chicken Veggie Wrap

Ingredients of Creamy Chicken Veggie Wrap

  • 1 cup boiled chicken
  • 1/2 cup cucumber
  • 1/2 cup tomato
  • 4 lettuce loose-leaf
  • 1/2 cup low fat mozzarella cheese
  • 3 tortillas
  • 1 cup hung curd
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 handfuls coriander leaves
  • salt as required
  • black pepper as required
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil
  • mixed herbs as required
  • chilli flakes as required
  • 2 tablespoon butter

How to make Creamy Chicken Veggie Wrap

Step 1

To begin with this easy recipe, wash and chop the veggies. Now, take a bowl and add in some lemon juice, paprika, salt, pepper and mixed herbs, whisk it well and add in the veggies, toss them up and keep aside.

Step 2

Meanwhile, take a pan and heat the tortillas. Once done, spread some butter and layer some lettuce leaves.

Step 3

Take a bowl and add in hung curd, chili flakes, salt and pepper, whisk it well with 1 tsp chili oil to make a homemade spread.

Step 4

Take the lettuce layered tortillas, add in the veggies followed by hung curd dip, spread it across the wrap, add grated cheese. Fold it up and bake or microwave it for 3-4 minutes to enjoy. You can also heat it on a pan and allow the cheese to melt and enjoy!
